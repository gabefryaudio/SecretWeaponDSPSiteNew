Canvas Comp

Draw your own compressor & waveshaper curves

Shape dynamics that traditional compressors can't touch. Draw curves that let snare transients explode while perfectly controlling the ring. Make 808s hit harder by compressing the sustain but expanding the attack. Create drum bus glue that tightens the kit without losing impact. Then use the waveshaper to add everything from tape-style saturation to brutal clipping. This is compression reimagined - where you literally draw the dynamics and harmonics you hear in your head.

Quantum EQ

Surgical precision meets musical transparency

Shape curves that sound as smooth as they look. Our asymmetric bell curves let you boost gently and cut precisely - like having a different EQ for boosting and cutting. Latest-generation algorithms deliver silky high-end boosts without harshness and natural, musical changes that sit perfectly in your mix. Watch the real-time analyzer dance at 120fps while you sculpt - seeing exactly what you're hearing makes all the difference.
